Welcome, Young Jedi.

At Scummerville Saber Arts, we invite you to immerse yourself in the thrilling world of lightsaber combat inspired by the iconic Star Wars franchise. For over 15 years, our academy has been dedicated to teaching the art of lightsaber dueling, from flourishes to form VI, we blend the rich lore of the Star Wars universe with real-world combat techniques.
